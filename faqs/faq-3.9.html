Missed Confirming Attendance/Forgot to Confirm Attendance: [Updated as of May-2024]

What to Do If You Missed Confirming Your Attendance for the Month, Ensuring Timely Completion of Attendance Procedures.


Forgetting to confirm your attendance for the month can disrupt the completion of attendance procedures. Here's what to do if you missed confirming your attendance:


1. Confirming Attendance Monthly in HRMS App:
• Confirming your attendance monthly in the HRMS app is a crucial part of HR protocols.
• Adhering to company protocols ensures timely completion of attendance procedures and accurate record-keeping.


2. HR Assistance:
• If you forgot to confirm your attendance for the month, HR can assist you in completing this process.
• HR may reach out to you to request confirmation or may confirm on your behalf without prior notice.


3. Self-Confirmation:
• It's advisable for employees to confirm their attendance by themselves whenever possible.
• Self-confirmation allows you to review your attendance records and identify any discrepancies that need to be addressed with HR.


By promptly confirming your attendance and adhering to company protocols, you can ensure timely completion of attendance procedures and maintain accurate attendance records..

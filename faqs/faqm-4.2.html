Manager Dashboard Tour Guide: [Updated as of May-2024]

An Overview of the Tools, Metrics, and Resources Available to Managers for Efficient Team Management


Welcome to your manager dashboard, where you'll find a suite of tools and resources to streamline team management. Here's an overview to help you navigate and utilize each section effectively:


1. Exploring the Manager Dashboard:
• Familiarize yourself with the layout and functionality to leverage its full potential in optimizing team performance.

2. Navigation Bar Elements:
• Navigate through the navigation bar elements to access essential sections such as the dashboard, employee list, leaves, and attendance.
• Each navigation bar element provides quick access to specific tools and metrics crucial for effective team management.



3. Dashboard Elements:
• Dive into the various elements present on your dashboard to gain insights into team dynamics and performance.
• Utilize features like the managed employees duty card to track assigned tasks and monitor progress.
• Keep an eye on the "Employees on Leave" and "Absentees" sections to stay informed about team availability and attendance.
• Manage leave requests efficiently by accessing the corresponding section to approve or reject requests promptly.


4. Making the Most Out of Each Section:
• Take advantage of the information presented in each section to make informed decisions and effectively manage your team.
• Utilize features such as employee lists to view detailed information about your team members, track leave requests, and monitor attendance records.


By leveraging the tools, metrics, and resources available on your manager dashboard, you can enhance team productivity, address resource allocation effectively, and foster a culture of accountability and performance within your team.

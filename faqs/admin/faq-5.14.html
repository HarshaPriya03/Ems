Step-by-Step Guide for a Pay Run: [Updated as of May-2024]

Follow a Systematic Process to Complete Each Stage of Payroll Processing, Including Data Input, Verification, and Payment Generation.


Step-by-Step Guide for a Pay Run involves following a systematic process to complete each stage of payroll processing, including data input, verification, and payment generation.



1. Access Payroll Dashboard: Navigate to the payroll section in the dashboard sidebar and click on it to open the payroll dashboard.



2. Launch Payroll Run: In the payroll dashboard, locate the "Launch Payroll Run" section, usually found in the right middle part of the page. Click on "Create Payrun" to initiate the pay run process.



3. Data Collection and Loading: The process begins with collecting all necessary data across the HRMS needed for the pay run. This includes employee attendance, leaves, bonuses, deductions, and other relevant information. It may take 5-10 seconds for the system to load initially.



4. Processing Payroll: After data collection, the system processes the payroll data, calculating salaries, deductions, taxes, and other components according to established pay schedules and company policies.


5. Run Payroll Button: After the initial loading, navigate to the "Run payroll" button located at the right top corner. Clicking on this button initiates further backend mapping processes, such as data allocation, package preparation, bundling, response generation, and delivery.



6. Step-by-Step Process: Once the pay run is initiated, you'll be guided through a series of steps to complete the payroll processing cycle. These steps typically include:
• Salary Details: Review the fixed salary component of every employee and confirm.
• Leave Adjustments: Fetch and mark any leave of pay deductions.
• Loan Repayments: Deduct loan EMIs from employee salaries.
• Other Deductions: Deduct miscellaneous deductions as necessary.
• Bonus Allocation: Allocate bonuses to eligible employees.
• Review Salary Structure: Review the entire payroll structure for accuracy.
• Confirm Salary: Confirm individual salary structures generated by the system.




7. Generate Statements: After completing all necessary steps, the system generates various statements, including pay slips, salary reports, tax deductions, and other relevant documents. Review these statements to ensure accuracy and compliance.


Following this systematic process ensures each stage of payroll processing is completed accurately, from data input to verification and payment generation.

Adding a New Leave Type: [Updated as of May-2024]

Customize Leave Categories to Cater to Diverse Employee Needs and Accommodate Various Types of Time Off Requests.


Adding a new leave type involves customizing leave categories to cater to diverse employee needs and accommodate various types of time off requests.
Here's a detailed guide on how to add a new leave type:


1.Adding a New Leave Type:
• Navigate to the Leave Management Tab: From the sidebar menu on the dashboard, access the leave management tab.
• Employee Leave Table: In the leave management section, locate the table displaying employee leave records.
• Accessing New Leave Type Option: Within the leave management interface, locate the navigation bar. Look for a button labeled "New Leave Type" and click on it.
• Form for Adding New Leave Type: Upon clicking the "New Leave Type" button, a form will appear where administrators can add details for the new leave type.
• Enter Leave Type Details: In the form, provide information about the new leave type, including the leave category name, description, accrual method (if applicable), maximum accrual limits, and any other relevant settings.
• Customize Settings: Customize the settings for the new leave type based on the specific requirements and policies of the organization, such as whether it requires approval, if it accrues over time, and any restrictions or eligibility criteria.
• Save Changes: After entering all the necessary details, save the changes to add the new leave type to the leave management system.
• Testing and Validation: Test the new leave type to ensure that it functions correctly within the leave management system and accurately reflects the organization's policies and practices.
• Employee Communication: Communicate the addition of the new leave type to employees, providing information about its availability, eligibility criteria, and any other relevant details.


By following these steps, organizations can customize leave categories to meet the diverse needs of their workforce and effectively manage various types of time off requests.

Checking Previous Months' Overall Attendance: [Updated as of May-2024]

Retrieve and Review Attendance Data from Previous Months for Historical Analysis and Reporting.


Checking Previous Months' Overall Attendance involves retrieving and reviewing attendance data from previous months for historical analysis and reporting purposes.



1. Access the Attendance Section: Navigate to the dashboard sidebar where the Attendance option is located. Click on it to access the attendance table.



2. Select Overall Attendance for Previous Months: In the attendance table, locate the navigation bar or dropdown menu where you can select different views or options. Look for the option to view overall attendance or monthly attendance.



3. Choose Previous Month and Year: Once you've located the overall attendance option, find the select month-year option. This allows you to specify the month and year for which you want to view the attendance data.



4. Select Month-Year: Click on the select month-year option and choose the desired month and year from the dropdown list. This will filter the attendance data to display information from the selected previous month.


5. Review Attendance Data: After selecting the previous month and year, review the attendance data displayed on the screen. Pay attention to details such as employee attendance records, total hours worked, absences, and any other relevant information.


By following these steps, you can easily retrieve and review attendance data from previous months, enabling historical analysis and reporting to track attendance trends over time.

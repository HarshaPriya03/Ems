Editing/Updating Employee Data: [Updated as of May-2024]

Ensure Accurate and Up-to-Date Employee Records to Support HR Processes, Compliance, and Decision-Making.


Ensuring accurate and up-to-date employee records through editing and updating employee data is crucial to support HR processes, compliance, and decision-making.
Here's a detailed guide on how to edit/update employee data:


1.Editing/Updating Employee Data:
• Navigate to the Employee Management Tab: From the sidebar menu on the dashboard, access the employee management tab.
• Employee Overview: In the employee management section, locate the table displaying employee details. Click on the "View More" button associated with the specific employee whose data needs to be updated.
• Initiating Edit Mode: On the employee overview tab, locate the "Edit" button at the top left corner. Click on the button to enter edit mode for the employee's details.
• Updating Employee Details: Once in edit mode, the user can modify the employee's information as necessary. This may include personal details, contact information, job title, department, or any other relevant data.
• Saving Changes: After making the required updates, save the changes to ensure that the employee's records are accurately updated.
• Reviewing Changes: Take a moment to review the edited employee data to ensure accuracy and completeness.
• Confirming Updates: Once satisfied with the changes, confirm the updates to finalize the editing process.
• Compliance Check: Ensure that any updates made comply with relevant HR policies, regulations, and data privacy laws.
• Decision Support: Updated employee data provides HR professionals and decision-makers with accurate information for workforce planning, performance management, and strategic decision-making.


By following these steps, organizations can maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records, supporting efficient HR processes, compliance requirements, and informed decision-making.

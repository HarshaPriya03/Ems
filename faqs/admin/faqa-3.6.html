Adding Approvers to the Leave Management System: [Updated as of May-2024]

Designate Authorized Personnel to Review and Approve Leave Requests to Maintain Accountability and Compliance With Organizational Policies.


Adding approvers to the leave management system involves designating authorized personnel to review and approve leave requests to maintain accountability and compliance with organizational policies.
Here's a detailed guide on how to add approvers to the leave management system:


1.Adding Approvers to the Leave Management System:
• Navigate to the Leave Management Tab: From the sidebar menu on the dashboard, access the leave management tab.
• Employee Leave Table: In the leave management section, locate the table displaying employee leave records.
• Accessing Add Approver Option: Within the leave management interface, locate the navigation bar. Look for a button labeled "Add Approver" and click on it.
• Form for Adding New Approver: Upon clicking the "Add Approver" button, a form will appear where administrators can add details for the new approver.
• Enter Approver Details: In the form, provide information about the new approver, including their name, contact information, and any other relevant details.
• Designating Approval Authority: Specify the approval authority of the new approver, such as which departments or teams they are responsible for approving leave requests for.
• Save Changes: After entering all the necessary details, save the changes to add the new approver to the leave management system.
• Testing and Validation: Test the new approver's access and permissions within the leave management system to ensure they can effectively review and approve leave requests.
• Communication: Inform employees and relevant stakeholders about the addition of the new approver, including any changes to the approval process or workflow.
• Training and Support: Offer training and support to the new approver on how to use the leave management system, including how to review and approve leave requests and handle any exceptions or special cases.


By following these steps, organizations can designate authorized personnel as approvers within the leave management system, ensuring accountability and compliance with organizational policies regarding leave approval processes.

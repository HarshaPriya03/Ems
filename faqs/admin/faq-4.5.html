Confirming Overall Monthly Attendance: [Updated as of May-2024]

Ensure Timely Confirmation of Monthly Attendance Records to Maintain Data Accuracy and Compliance.


Confirming Overall Monthly Attendance is crucial to ensure timely confirmation of monthly attendance records, maintaining data accuracy, and compliance.



1. Access Monthly Attendance Records: Navigate to the dashboard sidebar where the Attendance option is located. Click on it to access the attendance table.



2. Select Overall Attendance: On the navigation bar, locate the dropdown menu or list option. From the dropdown menu, select the "Overall Attendance" option. This will display the entire monthly attendance sheet for all employees.



3. Check Confirmation Status: In the attendance sheet, navigate to the last column titled "Confirmed by Employee." Here, you can see which employees have confirmed their attendance for the month and who hasn't.



4. Manual Confirmation by HR: If any employee hasn't confirmed their attendance, HR can manually confirm it on their behalf. To do this, navigate back to the dashboard and find the quick access option in the header.



5. Confirm Attendance Manually: In the quick access dropdown menu, select the "Confirm" option. This will open a table displaying employees who haven't confirmed their attendance.


6. Confirm Attendance for Unconfirmed Employees: Select the employees who haven't confirmed and manually confirm their attendance by checking the corresponding checkboxes.


7. Submit Confirmation: After selecting the employees, submit the confirmation. This action will ensure that the monthly attendance records are accurate and up to date.


By following these steps, you can effectively confirm overall monthly attendance, maintain data accuracy, and ensure compliance with attendance policies.

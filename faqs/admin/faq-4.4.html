Reviewing Total Biometric Punches in Attendance Log: [Updated as of May-2024]

Track and Analyze the Total Number of Biometric Punches Recorded for Accurate Attendance Monitoring.


Reviewing Total Biometric Punches in the Attendance Log is essential for tracking and analyzing the total number of biometric punches recorded, ensuring accurate attendance monitoring.



1. Navigate to Attendance Management: Access the dashboard sidebar where the Attendance option is located. Click on it to proceed to the Attendance Management section.



2. Review Biometric Punches: In the Attendance Log section, you'll find a comprehensive log of all biometric punch activities. This log typically includes details such as Date, Employee Name, In Time, and Out Time.



3. Analyze Biometric Punch Data: Once you've selected the Check-Out option, a form will appear where you can enter the employee's name, date, time, and reason for the manual check-Out. Ensure all details are accurate and relevant.


4. Address Incorrect Punches:Regularly review the biometric punch data to track employee attendance patterns and identify any discrepancies or anomalies. Look for instances of missing punches, incorrect punch times, or unauthorized entries.


5. Ensure Accuracy in Attendance Monitoring: Use the biometric punch data to ensure accuracy in attendance monitoring. Compare the recorded punches with the expected attendance records to identify any discrepancies and take appropriate measures to rectify them.


By following these steps and regularly checking and analyzing the biometric punch data in the Attendance Log, you can effectively track employee attendance, identify any issues or discrepancies, and ensure accurate attendance monitoring.

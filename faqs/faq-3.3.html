Viewing Month-Wise Attendance: [Updated as of May-2024]

Easily Navigate Through Your Attendance Data on a Month-to-Month Basis for Comprehensive Tracking and Analysis.


Easily navigating through your attendance data on a month-to-month basis allows for comprehensive tracking and analysis. Here's how you can access your month-wise attendance records:


1. Understanding Attendance and Monthly Records:
• Attendance records provide insights into your presence and absence during work hours, aiding in tracking and analysis.
• Monthly attendance records offer a summarized view of your attendance data for each month, facilitating easy tracking and analysis over time.



2. Accessing Your Monthly Attendance:
• Start by locating the attendance option in the sidebar of your dashboard and clicking on it.
• A page will open displaying an attendance table with navigation options.
• Look for the option to navigate to Monthly Attendance and click on it.



3. Navigating Through Monthly Records:
• Upon clicking, a new page will open with separate tabs for each month's attendance.
• By default, the current month's attendance table will be displayed.
• To view attendance data for previous months, locate the selection box labeled "Select Month-Year".
• Choose the desired month and year from the available options, and the corresponding month's attendance table will be displayed.



4. Reviewing Monthly Attendance:
• Review the monthly attendance tables to track your attendance patterns, identify trends, and analyze your overall attendance performance.
• Pay attention to any deviations or irregularities in attendance and take necessary actions to address them.


By following these steps and regularly reviewing your month-wise attendance records, you can effectively track your attendance performance and make informed decisions to improve your attendance habits.

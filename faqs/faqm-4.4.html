Approving Leaves for Managed Employees: [Updated as of May-2024]

Step-by-Step Guide to Reviewing and Approving Leave Requests Submitted by Your Team Members.


As a manager, overseeing leave requests from your team members is an important aspect of your role. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to review and approve leave requests submitted by your team:



1. Navigating to Leave Management:
• Begin by accessing the leave management feature on your dashboard. You can find this option in the sidebar menu.
• Click on the "Leaves" option to proceed to the leave management section.



2. Reviewing Leave Requests:
• In the leaves table, you'll see a list of leave requests submitted by your team members.
• Look for the "Leave Status" column to identify requests that are pending your approval. If a request is pending at the manager level, it will be indicated here.



3. Viewing Leave Details:
• To review the details of a leave request, click on the "View Details" option corresponding to the request you wish to review.
• A new page will open displaying comprehensive details of the leave request, including the employee's name, type of leave, requested dates, and any additional notes provided by the employee.



4. Taking Action:
• After reviewing the leave details, you'll have the option to take action on the request.
• Click on the "Set Action" button to initiate the action. A popup window will appear with options to "Approve," "Reject," or "Send to Approver" (if applicable).
• Select the appropriate action based on your review of the request. You can also provide any remarks or comments if necessary.
• Once you've made your selection, click on the "Submit" button to confirm your decision.


By following these steps, you can efficiently review and approve leave requests from your team members, ensuring smooth management of leave schedules and maintaining productivity within your team.

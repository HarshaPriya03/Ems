Understanding Your Managerial Landing Page: [Updated as of May-2024]

A Walkthrough of the Features and Functions Available Upon Logging In to Your Manager Account.


Upon logging into your manager account, exploring the dashboard provides valuable insights and tools for effective management. Here's a breakdown to help you navigate your managerial landing page:


1. Exploring the Manager Dashboard:
• Your manager dashboard serves as a central hub for accessing key features and information related to your managerial responsibilities.
• Understanding the layout and functionality of the dashboard empowers you to efficiently oversee and manage your team.



2. Sidebar Elements:
• The sidebar offers quick access to essential sections such as the dashboard, employee list, leaves, and attendance.
• Each sidebar element provides access to specific functions and data related to managing your team's activities.



3. Dashboard Elements:
• Explore the various elements on your dashboard, including widgets, charts, and summary sections.
• These elements provide valuable insights into team performance, leave statuses, attendance trends, and more.


4. Making the Most Out of Each Section:
• Take advantage of the information presented in each section to make informed decisions and effectively manage your team.
• Utilize features such as employee lists to view detailed information about your team members, track leave requests, and monitor attendance records.


By familiarizing yourself with the features and functions available on your manager dashboard, you can streamline your management tasks and enhance productivity within your team.
